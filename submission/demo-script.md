# ResourceFlow Demo Script

## 2-minute demo

### 0:00 to 0:20

Introduce the problem:

"Community response teams still coordinate through calls, spreadsheets, and scattered messages. That makes it hard to know what is urgent, who is available, and where limited volunteers should go first."

### 0:20 to 0:45

Open `index.html` and explain that ResourceFlow centralizes requests, volunteers, assignments, and analytics in one place.

Click `Load Demo`.

### 0:45 to 1:15

Move to `Operations`.

Show:

- active requests
- urgency levels
- generated assignments
- coordinator guidance

Mention that the matching engine considers urgency, skills, zone, transport, and experience.

### 1:15 to 1:35

Move to `Volunteer Portal`.

Add one volunteer and explain how the platform can onboard responders with skills and availability.

### 1:35 to 1:55

Move to `AI Insights`.

Show:

- next action
- recruitment gap
- fairness watch
- generated executive brief

### 1:55 to 2:00

Close with:

"ResourceFlow helps communities respond faster, coordinate smarter, and scale support using a clear, data-driven workflow."
