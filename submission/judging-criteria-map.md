# Judging Criteria Mapping

## Technical Merit (40%)

- Firebase-ready storage layer with local fallback
- Google Maps dispatch links for request zones
- PWA manifest and service worker packaging
- explainable volunteer scoring and structured JSON export
- multi-page app structure for maintainability and scale

## User Experience (10%)

- clear navigation with separate overview, operations, volunteer, insights, and judge pages
- labeled forms and large touch targets
- skip link and visible keyboard focus states
- live status and plain-language summaries

## Alignment With Cause (25%)

- directly addresses fragmented coordination in food, medical, shelter, and logistics response
- tracks projected beneficiaries, coverage, critical fill rate, and fairness across zones
- supports measurable community impact storytelling

## Innovation And Creativity (25%)

- AI-style staffing simulation lab
- multilingual outreach message generation
- fairness watch and underserved-zone detection
- judge-ready executive brief and pitch narrative generation
