# ResourceFlow Pitch Outline

## Slide 1

Problem:
Volunteer coordination is fragmented and slow during critical community operations.

## Slide 2

Who suffers:
- NGOs
- local coordinators
- volunteers
- beneficiaries waiting for support

## Slide 3

Solution:
ResourceFlow centralizes requests, volunteers, matching, and analytics.

## Slide 4

Product demo:
- request intake
- volunteer portal
- automated matching
- AI insight workspace

## Slide 5

Why it works:
- urgency-aware
- skills-aware
- zone-aware
- transport-aware
- scalable with Firebase

## Slide 6

Impact:
- faster staffing
- better coverage
- fewer coordination gaps
- stronger service delivery

## Slide 7

Future:
- Firebase real-time sync
- Google Maps routing
- Gemini summaries
- notification automation
